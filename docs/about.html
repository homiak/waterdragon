About Dragan-uisge

Learn more about the lore and development of the Water Dragon mod.

Dragan-uisge (Water Dragon) is a mod inspired by Celtic mythology and Scottish legends. It brings the mystical element of Water Dragons to the world of Minetest, enriching gameplay with unique creatures and magic.

The mod is developed by the community and welcomes contributions from enthusiasts around the world.
