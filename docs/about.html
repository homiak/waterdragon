About Dragan-uisge

Dragan-uisge is a fantasy mod for the voxel game engine Luanti. It introduces legendary Water Dragons, elemental magic, and a lore-driven experience. Learn more about the features.

Designed for roleplayers, explorers, and modders alike, Dragan-uisge invites you to write your own stories within a dynamic, mysterious world.

No in-game purchases, no ads. Just legends.
