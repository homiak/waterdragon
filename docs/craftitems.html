Craftitems and Tools

Discover the unique items you can craft in the Dragan-uisge mod.

Drops and Loot

Water Dragon Scales

Water Dragon Scales are used to craft powerful armor and weapons, enhancing the player's abilities in combat. These can be obtained from defeating Water Dragons. The older the Dragon is - the more chance for more scales.

Water Dragon Bones

Water Dragon Bones are used in various crafting recipes as a handle for pickaxes, swords, axes and shovels. They also could be obtained by defeating Water Dragons.

Dragon Water Drops

Dragon Water Drops are a rare resource that can be collected from Water Dragons, and can be used for reviving Dragons, and other animals. If the animal dies, they completely recover the animal's strength and abilities. To revive a fallen creature, right-click it while holding this item.

Dragon Horns

Dragon Horns can be used in transporting and calling Dragons that are bonded to it. To bond a Dragon to a Dragon Horn, right-click the Dragon while holding the Horn. If you right-click a Dragon while the Horn is bonded to it, the Horn will capture the Dragon. To summon it back, simply hold the Horn and right-click while looking at a block. To call a Dragon from far away, right-click the sky while holding the Horn. If a Dragon who is linked to a Horn dies, the data of the Dragon will be cleared and the Horn will no longer be able to summon it.

Wing Horns

Wing Horns are special horns, located on the elbows of the wings of Dragons. If you get one, you will be able to throw it, and the Wing Horn will damage any enemies while in the air.

Dragon Teeth

Dragon Teeth are not just drops; they have unique properties to command your level of being a Dragon Rider. If you use it multiple times (right-click a mob) and if Dragons trust you, they may even give you the title of a Dragon Rider, giving the privilege of commanding them. Or more simply said: they will give you the privilege "reileadair_an_dhragain", which means "Dragon Rider".

Dragon Eyes

Dragon Eyes allow you to project your vision into a free-floating, invisible camera. To activate, right-click while holding the eye and looking at the sky. Your view will detach from your body, allowing you to fly freely and pass through walls as a spectator. This is perfect for scouting caves, exploring hidden areas, or observing the world from any angle. Be aware that your physical body remains stationary and vulnerable while in this mode. To return to your body, right-click the sky again.

Spawner Eggs and other craftitems

Creative Rare Water Dragon Eggs

Creative Rare Water Dragon Eggs are used to spawn full-grown and wild Rare Water Dragons in the world. Pretty good for testing.

Creative Pure Water Dragon Eggs

Creative Pure Water Dragon Eggs can spawn full-grown and wild Pure Water Dragons in the world. Ideal for testing and exploration.

Buckets of Dragons Water

Buckets of Dragons Water are crafted from Dragon Water Drops. They are capable of taming creatures, such as Dragons and other mythical beasts. To tame them, right-click them while holding this item.

Books about Water Dragons

Books about Water Dragons are more than just a guide; it's a living bestiary whose secrets are only revealed to a true Dragon Rider. Its enchanted pages will remain blank until you prove your worth. The journey begins with your first right-click on a Water Dragon, unlocking the initial chapters. As you deepen your bond—discovering different species (by right-clicking a Scottish Dragon), taking to the skies on Dragonback, and even mastering the art of forging with Dragon Water (drop a boulder block on the ground and have your Dragon's breath on it) — the book will magically fill with new knowledge, guiding you on your path to becoming a legendary "Reileadair an Dhragain", the Dragon Rider.

Draconic Steel

Draconic Steel allows you to forge powerful tools and armor linked to a specific Water Dragon. The power of these items is directly tied to your proximity to the bonded Dragon — the closer you are, the more effective your tools will be. However, if the Dragon that the tool is bonded to dies, the tool will lose its power and shatter.

Draconic Steel Ingots

Draconic Steel Ingots are used to create various powerful tools and armour made from Draconic Steel. Each ingot is linked to the Dragon who forged it, and ingots forged from different Dragons cannot be crafted together.

Draconic Steel Armour

Draconic Steel Armour provides enhanced protection and is crafted using Draconic Steel Ingots. The armour's effectiveness increases with the bond to the Dragon that forged it. Rare Water-Forged Draconic Steel Armour is more formidable, because of Rare Water Dragons being more powerful than the others.

Draconic Steel Tools

Draconic Steel Tools are crafted using Draconic Steel Ingots and provide enhanced durability and effectiveness in various tasks. Draconic Steel Pickaxes can break any type of block, while Draconic Steel Swords are known for their massive damage and durability points.

The Dragonstone Crucible

The Dragonstone Crucible is a special crafting station used to forge Draconic Steel Ingots. Learn how to forge Draconic Steel Ingots here.
