Forging Draconic Steel

Learn how to create powerful tools bonded to your Dragons.

Building the Dragon Forge

To forge Draconic Steel, you must first build a special Dragon Forge. Follow these steps to construct it:

Step 1: The Base Platform

Place a 3x3 base of Dragonstone Bricks (waterdragon:dragonstone_bricks_ .. rare_water or pure_water).

Step 2: The Inner Stone

Place another 3x3 platform of Dragonstone Blocks on the first platform (waterdragon:dragonstone_block_ .. pure_water or rare_water) and the pillars to support the structure, also with Dragonstone Blocks.

Step 3: The Chimney Base number 1

Place Dragonstone Blocks to connect the pillars.

Step 4: The Chimney Base number 2

Place the side pillars using Dragonstone Blocks to support the forge structure. And place four pillars above the structure to create a base for a chimney.

Step 5: Outer Shell

Place the Dragonstone Bricks in a circular pattern to form the outer shell of the forge. Then, place a Dragon Forge node (waterdragon:draconic_forge_ .. pure_water or rare_water) in the center of the structure, and your forge is complete!

Forging Ingots

Once your Forge is complete, you can start forging the Draconic Steel Ingots. First, have your Dragon's breath into the Forge (check if the type is the same as the type of the Forge. For example: a Rare Water Dragon cannot forge an ingot if you have built a Pure Water Forge!). Wait a few seconds (something like 15-20) and your Ingot has been forged! If you see that it cools down, the Crucible is not full, keep your Dragon's breath into the Forge. Make sure to monitor the process closely to ensure successful forging.
