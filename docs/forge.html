Forging Draconic Steel

Learn how to create powerful tools bonded to your Dragons.

To forge Draconic Steel, you must first build a special Dragon Forge. This structure requires rare materials found throughout the world. Once the forge is built, you can use it to infuse standard steel with the essence of a Water Dragon.

The process requires you to place a steel ingot in the forge, along with a scale from the specific Dragon you wish to bond the item to. The forge will then consume the items and produce a Draconic Steel Ingot, forever linked to that Dragon.
