Hatching Water Dragon Eggs

Learn how to bring your own Water Dragon into the world.

To hatch a Water Dragon Egg, place it on top of a platform made from 3x3 Wet Bone Pile nodes (waterdragon:bone_pile_wet). Then, place a water source anywhere on the platform and wait for about 20 minutes. A young Water Dragon will emerge!

Steps to hatch a Water Dragon Egg

Step 1: Place the Egg

Place the Water Dragon Egg on the platform and ensure it is in a suitable environment.

Step 2: the Water Source

Place a water source block on the platform to initiate the hatching process.

Step 3: Time

Wait for about 20 minutes for the Water Dragon to hatch. Make sure that you stand next to it, so that the Dragon can sense your presence.

Step 4: Enjoy Your New Dragon!

Once the Water Dragon hatches, it will need your care and attention. Make sure to feed it and provide a safe environment.
